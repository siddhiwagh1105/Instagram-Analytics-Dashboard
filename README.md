# Instagram Analytics Dashboard – Power BI Project

## Dashboard Title

**Instagram Performance & Engagement Analytics Dashboard**

---

# Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a complete analysis of Instagram content performance using key engagement and audience metrics. The dashboard helps identify high-performing content categories, best posting times, audience reach trends, traffic sources, and follower growth patterns.

The project is designed for:

* Social Media Analysts
* Digital Marketing Teams
* Content Creators
* Brand Managers
* Influencer Marketing Analysis

---

# Dashboard Objectives

* Track Instagram engagement performance
* Analyze reach and follower growth
* Identify the best posting schedule
* Compare content categories and media types
* Understand audience traffic sources
* Improve content strategy using data-driven insights

---

# Key KPIs

### 1. Average Reach

Measures the average number of users reached by Instagram posts.

**Business Insight:**
Helps evaluate overall content visibility and audience exposure.

---

### 2. Average Engagement Rate

Shows the average engagement generated across posts.

**Business Insight:**
Used to measure how effectively content interacts with the audience.

---

### 3. Average Followers Gained

Displays the average number of followers gained through content performance.

**Business Insight:**
Helps identify whether content contributes to audience growth.

---

# Visualizations & Analytics Description

## 1. Engagement Rate Trend Analysis (Line Chart)

**Visualization Type:** Line Chart

**Description:**
Tracks engagement rate trends over time to identify performance fluctuations and audience interaction patterns.

**Insights Generated:**

* Detect high-performing periods
* Understand engagement consistency
* Monitor audience activity trends

---

## 2. Engagement Rate by Day of Week (Clustered Bar Chart)

**Visualization Type:** Bar Chart

**Description:**
Compares average engagement rates across different weekdays.

**Insights Generated:**

* Identify best posting days
* Optimize weekly content scheduling
* Improve audience interaction timing

---

## 3. Reach by Media Type (Donut Chart)

**Visualization Type:** Donut Chart

**Description:**
Analyzes how different media formats contribute to overall audience reach.

**Media Types Included:**

* Reels
* Carousel Posts
* Images
* Videos

**Insights Generated:**

* Discover the most effective content format
* Compare performance between media types
* Improve content strategy allocation

---

## 4. Reach by Posting Hour (Ribbon Chart)

**Visualization Type:** Ribbon Chart

**Description:**
Shows how posting time impacts audience reach.

**Insights Generated:**

* Identify peak audience activity hours
* Determine optimal posting schedule
* Maximize content visibility

---

## 5. Likes by Content Category (Clustered Bar Chart)

**Visualization Type:** Bar Chart

**Description:**
Compares likes generated across different content categories.

**Possible Categories:**

* Fashion
* Lifestyle
* Education
* Entertainment
* Travel
* Technology

**Insights Generated:**

* Identify top-performing content themes
* Improve content planning strategy
* Understand audience preferences

---

## 6. Traffic Source Analysis (Treemap)

**Visualization Type:** Treemap

**Description:**
Displays contribution of various traffic sources to total reach.

**Traffic Sources Included:**

* Explore Page
* Hashtags
* Home Feed
* Profile Visits
* External Sources

**Insights Generated:**

* Understand where audience traffic originates
* Evaluate discoverability performance
* Optimize traffic acquisition strategy
